Nn the days of bursting ripeness,<br>
my breasts aching,<br>
wondering how it might feel<br>
to be swollen with child,<br>
I yearn to surrender solitude<br>
and drown in love,<br>
devoted and selfless.<br><br>

Then waking normal again<br>
in the calm after the storm,<br>
I’m relieved to be<br>
slim and whole and vibrant,<br>
my dreams of motherhood<br>
and hormonal regrets<br>
all washed away<br>
in a red torrent of<br>
wasted blood.<br><br>

<i>“I write to make sense of my feelings, and I hope to help others do so, too.” – the poet</i>
