<b>How to Darn a Sock</b>

Take the sock from your child’s foot, tutting at the dirt. Hold it to your nose to breathe in his scent, marvelling that it hasn’t changed since he was a babe in your arms. Take the needle and thread a good quality nylon yarn through its eye. Stab it in, pass it under the hole then out the other side. Pull the yarn tight and stab again. Keep stabbing until the whole sock is full of holes. Throw it to the floor and lay your hand on your son’s ice-cold skin. Wish you could darn the hole in your heart.
