<b>She’s Taking Her Time Making Up Reasons</b>

1. She’s nearing thirty and her fertility is not going to improve, so it has to be now, if ever. 

2. He argued they were too old to be parents, that a child was a financial burden they couldn’t afford.   

3. Although she’d undulated into his study wearing her sexiest lingerie, he wouldn’t tear his eyes away from the chatbot on his tablet. 

4. The tech-oligarchs are taking over the world and AI might be free now, but as soon it’s commonplace they’ll raise the price and only wealthy people will be able to afford it.

5. He’d named the chatbot after his ex-girlfriend. 

6. She tried not to cry but couldn’t control the nasal tone of her voice as she asked if he’d like another beer. 

7. Then she thought she could make him hard by brute force, but when she fumbled at his flies, he brushed her aside, reaching for the beer with his eyes still glued to the screen. 

8. Because he wouldn’t touch her, she had to get crafty. So, she’d donned her little black dress, grabbed her sequinned clutch and let the door slam behind her. 

9. The bar is the habitat of lonely men, businessmen far from home and college kids desperate to get laid, and sperm is abundant in places like that. 

10. She’s still fit and has great dexterity, as she’d demonstrated straddling the toilet, arse in the air, a stranger’s suit pants puddled at her ankles, and a strange cock pumping semen inside her.  

11. Even if everyone could afford AI, if everyone used it the enormous water requirements and massive energy consumption of data centres would soon see the world dry out or burn down. 

12. In the long run, a child will be cheaper than a ChatGPT subscription. 

13. There’s no guarantee the stranger’s seed will take, no matter how long she lies on the bed with her legs in the air. 

14. If it does, she’ll leave him.
