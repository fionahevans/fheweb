--- title: "Flow" author: "Fiona H Evans" date: 2022-04-27T00:00:00Z fontsize: 12pt css: "styles.css" categories: ["Poetry"] tags: ["Poetry"] ---

Days of bursting ripeness
breasts swollen and aching
wondering how it might feel
to be swollen with child
yearning to surrender solitude
and be drowned in love
devoted and selfless.

Then waking normal again
slim and whole and vibrant
in the calm after the storm
when pain washes away
lost dreams of motherhood
and hormonal regrets
in a red torrent of
wasted blood.

History

I wrote this poem, “Flow”, in 2005.
