I created my Twitter profile to enter a competition run by Ben Warden as @FlashSerious. I entered two Twitter-length stories with the hashtag #SFFiction and was over the moon when I was announced as one of the winners on 25 April 2022. You can read my winning story on Twitter.

Today, my story was was published in an anthology that you can buy from Amazon: 2022 Anthology (Volume 8) or Volumes 1-8 in a single book.
