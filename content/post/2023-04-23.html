I’ve just returned home from a wonderful Writers’ Retreat run by the [Society of Women Writers Western Australia] (https://www.swwofwa.com.au/). I’ve been a member for some years now, and this is the third retreat that I’ve been on. We stayed at th St. John of God Retreat Centre which sits within serene gardens that are home to a labyrinth and reflection water wall. All of our meals were catered and we had nothing to do but learn, write, commune and be at peace.
